The Free Automation Audit

A complimentary diagnostic that maps your workflow, identifies automation opportunities, and produces a build plan. No cost, no strings attached.

Request Your Audit

What the Audit Is

The Automation Audit is a focused discovery session where we map your current workflow, identify what's costing you time, and design a system to replace it. You'll walk away with a clear picture of what to automate, how much it costs, and whether it makes sense to proceed.

Free

What You Get

Four deliverables that give you clarity on what to build and what it costs.

Workflow Map

Visual diagram of your current state and proposed future state. See exactly what changes.

Prioritized Opportunities

List of automation candidates ranked by impact and complexity. Know where to start.

System Architecture

Proposed tools and integrations. We recommend the right stack for your workflow.

Build Scope & Quote

Timeline and price range for implementation. No surprises.

What You Need to Provide

To make the Audit useful, we need a few things from you.

  • Access to observe your current workflow (screen share or walkthrough)
  • Description of the manual steps you want to replace
  • List of tools you currently use
  • Rough volume (how often this workflow runs)
  • Your desired outcome (what does success look like?)
  • 30–60 minutes for a discovery call
