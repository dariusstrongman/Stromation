Quick Tips

  • Smile when you talk - they can hear it in your voice
  • Stand up - gives you more energy
  • Its ok if they say no - just be friendly and move on
  • Dont read word for word - use the script as a guide, be natural
  • Goal: get them to say yes to a free 15-min call with Darius. Thats it.

Step-by-Step Call Script (tap each step as you go)

STEP 1: OPEN

"Hi, is this the owner or manager?"
(Wait for them to answer. If they say no, ask "Is there a good time to reach them?")

STEP 2: INTRO

"Hey, my name is YOUR NAME. Im calling from Stromation. We work with local BUSINESS TYPE businesses to help automate things like appointment reminders, follow-ups, and scheduling so you dont have to do it all by hand."

STEP 3: THE QUESTION

"Im just curious - is there anything in your day-to-day that takes up way more time than it should? Like chasing people down, sending reminders, stuff like that?"
(Let them talk. Whatever they say, respond with "Yeah thats really common" or "I hear that a lot.")

STEP 4: THE ASK

"So what we do is a free 15-minute automation audit. Our founder Darius will look at your workflow and show you exactly what can be automated. No cost, no commitment. Would you be open to that?"

IF THEY SAY YES

"Great. Whats the best email to send the booking link to?"

(Write down their email. Then say:)

"Perfect, Ill send that over right now. Darius will take great care of you. Thanks for your time."

IF THEY SAY NOT RIGHT NOW

"Totally understand. Can I send you a quick email with some info? That way you have it whenever youre ready."

(If they give email, great. If not:)

"No worries at all. If anything changes, our website is stromation.com. Have a great day."

IF YOU GET VOICEMAIL

"Hi, this is YOUR NAME calling from Stromation. We help local businesses automate their scheduling, follow-ups, and admin work. We are offering a free 15-minute automation audit right now. You can book at stromation.com/audit or just give me a call back at 855-932-0493. Thanks, have a great day."
