service categories

Five categories. Each one scoped to your exact workflow during the free audit.

intake-leads.workflow

Intake & Lead Handling

Capture leads from any source, route them to the right place, and trigger follow-up sequences automatically. No more leads slipping through or slow responses.

common automations:
Form submission > database/CRM entry > email/SMS follow-up
Lead scoring and routing based on criteria
Appointment booking with calendar sync
Multi-channel intake (website, email, phone) unified
Typical outcome: Faster response times, no missed leads, and a clear view of your pipeline.
> typical build: 1-2 weeks
ops-admin.workflow

Ops & Admin Workflow Replacement

Replace the copy/paste, manual entry, and tool-hopping that eats your day. We connect your systems and automate the handoffs.

common automations:
Invoice/estimate data extraction into spreadsheets or accounting tools
Project handoffs between team members with task creation
Document generation from templates
Multi-step approval workflows with notifications
Typical outcome: Fewer errors, less admin time, and clean handoffs between steps.
> typical build: 2-3 weeks
support-ai.workflow

Customer Support & Knowledge Assistants

Deploy AI-powered assistants that handle common questions, triage requests, and escalate to humans when needed. Reduce inbox overload.

common automations:
AI chatbot trained on your FAQs and documentation
Email triage with tagging, prioritization, and draft replies
Ticket routing based on request type
Knowledge base search with AI-powered answers
Typical outcome: Faster responses for customers, less repetitive work for your team.
> typical build: 2-4 weeks
reporting.workflow

Reporting & Visibility

Stop spending hours gathering data for reports. We automate the collection, compilation, and delivery so you have visibility without the work.

common automations:
Scheduled reports pulled from multiple sources
Dashboards that update automatically
Alert notifications when metrics hit thresholds
Summary digests delivered to Slack, email, or SMS
Typical outcome: Always-current visibility into what matters, without manual data work.
> typical build: 1-2 weeks
