Real Estate Lead Generation
Live System

AI-Powered Real Estate Lead Engine for 33 DFW Cities

Problem

A growing DFW real estate operation needed to capture buyer and seller leads from 33 cities simultaneously with zero manual outreach. Leads from FSBO listings on Craigslist were being missed entirely. There was no automated follow-up, no lead scoring, no voicemail-to-lead pipeline, and no way to route qualified leads to agents in real time.

What We Built

A full-stack lead generation and nurturing system running 24/7 with 11 automated workflows, 89 landing pages, and a 10-table CRM database — all built in roughly two weeks.

  • Lead capture & validation
  • AI lead qualification (GPT-4o)
  • AI reply handler with bounce filtering
  • Smart agent routing
  • Agent referral emails
  • FSBO scraper (Craigslist)
  • Blog auto-publisher
  • Twitter auto-poster
  • Voicemail transcription (Whisper)
  • SMS handler (Twilio)
  • AI lead scoring (0–100 scale)

Workflow Map

Landing page form → Webhook capture → Supabase lead record → GPT-4o qualification → Lead scoring (0–100) → Agent routing → Automated follow-up email → Reply handling → Appointment booking. In parallel: Craigslist FSBO scraper → Contact extraction → Outreach → Reply monitoring. Voicemail → Whisper transcription → Lead creation.

Real Outcomes

System went from zero to fully operational in 14 days. 89 SEO-optimized landing pages covering every major DFW city. 11 n8n workflows running autonomously with an 83% success rate across 277+ executions. 76 FSBO listings scraped and contacted automatically from Craigslist. 30 AI-powered conversations handled without human intervention. 138,000+ property records indexed in the CRM pipeline. Full GA4 conversion tracking across all form pages.

Stack

n8n (workflow engine), Supabase (database + auth), OpenAI GPT-4o-mini (qualification + emails), Whisper (voice transcription), Twilio (SMS + voicemail), GitHub Pages (hosting), GA4 (analytics)

Problem

Missed calls during busy hours meant lost leads. No system to capture and follow up with callers who didn't leave voicemail.
