Questions

What trades does it support?

Currently optimized for Division 27 (Communications) -- structured cabling, security cameras, access control, AV/paging, and network infrastructure. More trades coming soon.

How accurate are the AI estimates?

When blueprints are available, the AI scans actual construction drawings and counts devices. Accuracy depends on plan quality, but our users report 90-95% accuracy on well-documented projects. You can always adjust counts before sending.

What if the blueprints are not available?

The system can estimate from project description, building type, and square footage using industry standards. These estimates are marked as "GPT Estimate" so you know the confidence level.

Can I use my own pricing?

Yes. You set your vendor prices, labor rates, overhead, profit margins, and sales tax rate. The system uses your numbers for every quote.

Does it work with PlanHub and ConstructConnect?

Yes. The system auto-monitors PlanHub for new bid invitations and can process ConstructConnect alerts. New projects appear in your dashboard automatically.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Monthly billing, cancel anytime. No contracts, no setup fees.
