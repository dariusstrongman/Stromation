The Signal in the Noise

Artificial Intelligence is moving faster than any technology in human history. But the media covering it is broken.

Most AI news falls into two categories: dense academic papers that business leaders can't decipher, or clickbait hype promising that "everything changes tomorrow."

Stromation was built to be the bridge. We provide the fastest, most accurate, and most practical AI news for operators, founders, and professionals who need to understand what's happening—and what to actually do about it.

We are not a news aggregator. We are a product studio that ships real AI products, and we share the one story a day that actually matters for builders and operators.

Who Runs This

Stromation is built by Darius Stroman, a founder who ships his own AI products — including ATSHack (AI resume grading) — and runs the automation studio behind it.

The daily pick is selected by an automated pipeline Darius built: it reads Hacker News, TechCrunch, VentureBeat, and The Verge every morning, scores every story from the last 24 hours, and picks the one a builder actually needs. The analysis and "the move" come from that same builder lens — when we say something matters, it is because it survived contact with a real business, not because a press release said so.

Our Editorial Standard

1. No Hype: We don't use words like "insane" or "mind-blowing." We report the facts and the implications.

2. Business Focus: If a new model drops, we don't just report the parameter count. We report how it changes the unit economics of your business.

3. The Move: Every story comes with "The Move"—the exact action you should take based on the news.

4. Built by Builders: Our analysis is driven by our experience actually building and shipping AI products.

5. Real Numbers Only: We are early and growing. You will never see an inflated subscriber count or a made-up stat on this site — if we cite a number, it is real.
