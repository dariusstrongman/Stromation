About Stromation

Stromation is a workflow automation studio founded by Darius Stroman. With a background in IT systems, automation, and building reliable workflows, Darius started Stromation after seeing too many business owners buried in repetitive admin work — and too many "automation solutions" that broke the moment something changed.

We take a different approach: understand your actual process, build something that fits, and make sure it keeps running.
