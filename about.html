The Founder

About Stromation

Stromation is a workflow automation studio founded by Darius, a software engineer with a Master of Science in Software Engineering and over a decade of hands-on experience in IT systems, network infrastructure, and process automation. He has built and maintained workflows for operations ranging from small businesses to enterprise environments.

After years of watching businesses burn hours on manual data entry, copy-paste reporting, and broken handoffs between tools, he started Stromation to offer what most "automation agencies" don't: systems that are built to your actual process, properly tested, and designed to keep running without babysitting.

We take a different approach: understand your actual process, build something that fits, and make sure it keeps running.

Our Philosophy

Replace steps, not jobs. Automation should handle the boring, repetitive work so you can focus on the parts that actually need a human. We're not here to make things complicated. We're here to make things work.

Direct Communication

You work directly with the founder, not a revolving door of account managers. One point of contact who knows your system.

Tool-Agnostic

We use n8n, custom APIs, and AI tools. Whatever fits your workflow best. No vendor lock-in, no upselling software you don't need.

Built to Last

Every system is documented, tested, and handed off with clear runbooks. If something breaks, you'll know why and how to fix it.

Fast Turnaround

Most automations are scoped, built, and deployed within 1–3 weeks. No 6-month enterprise timelines for things that should take days.
